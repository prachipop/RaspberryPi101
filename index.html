RaspberryPi 101

What is Raspberry Pi?




RaspberryPi is a low cost, credit card sized computer.

It's a platform for interactive devices and the Internet of Things. GPIO pins and HAT extensions make it easy to add sensors, physical controls, outputs and interfaces to your projects.

How to build a RaspberryPi



What you will need:





    A monitor with a cable

    (and, if needed, an HDMI adaptor)







    A USB keyboard







    A USB mouse







    Power supply







    An SD card







    And ofcourse, your RaspberryPi along with the Raspbian Software installed via NOOBS.









First install the Micro SD card in the back, as shown in the image below:




Then plug in the HDMI




Plug in a USB Mouse




Plug in a USB Keyboard




And finally plug in the Power adapter to Micro USB







This screen (NOOBS) allows us to choose an operating system to install. These cards come preinstalled, but a fresh setup would require moving some files onto the SD card. Choose Raspbian and click Install!
